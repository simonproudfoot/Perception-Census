Welcome to the Perception Census!

You are about to take part in one one of the largest scientific studies on neurodiversity ever undertaken. Brought together by a team of scientists and philosophers from the Universities of Sussex and Glasgow, you’ll learn about perception and explore how differently we each experience the world around us.

Let’s get started

From your laptop or desktop computer, you’ll complete a series of fun interactive tasks exploring colour, music, time, illusion and more.

What will be discovered? What will we learn? Your participation, along with thousands of others, could rewrite how we understand the world around us. Initial results will be revealed this Autumn.
